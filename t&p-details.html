Online Campus Selection System – Academic Project

The Online Campus Selection System is a web-based platform developed as part of my MCA Semester-3 academic project. Its primary purpose is to streamline campus placement processes by enabling Training & Placement (T&P) departments to manage student registrations, company listings, and selection rounds in an organized manner.

The system offers two main roles: Administrator and Student. Administrators can manage company details, job postings, and selection schedules, while students can register, upload resumes, apply to jobs, and track their status through a personalized dashboard.

Additional features include company-wise student shortlisting, automated notification handling, and historical data access. The platform ensures efficient communication between students and placement officers while maintaining structured records of campus hiring activities.

This project helped me strengthen skills in role-based authentication, form validation, CRUD operations, and academic-level project documentation. It demonstrated my ability to design user-centric systems aligned with real-world institutional workflows.

Technologies Used: HTML5, JavaScript, CSS, Bootstrap, JQuery, ASP.NET, SQL

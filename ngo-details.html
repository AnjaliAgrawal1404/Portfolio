NGO Awareness Projects – Web Demos for Deepak Foundation

As part of my four-week social internship at Deepak Foundation, I designed and developed two informative demo websites aimed at promoting social welfare initiatives and COVID-19 awareness. This internship was conducted under the BCA curriculum at Navrachana University.

The first project was a COVID-19 awareness demo website created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It highlighted the foundation’s pandemic relief work and initiatives. Based on feedback, I iteratively improved the design by adding Bootstrap components and multimedia content to make the site more user-friendly and impactful.

The second project was a Samaj Suraksha Sankul demo website, built after in-depth research and communication with the NGO. I contributed to its content structure, created the "About Us" page, designed a site flowchart, and submitted the visual blueprint. These websites were developed in collaboration with teammates and submitted for review during our internship presentation.

Throughout this project, I gained valuable skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, content structuring, professional communication, and time management while learning to work within real-world constraints and organizational requirements.

Technologies Used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap
